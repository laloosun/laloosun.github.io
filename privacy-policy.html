Privacy Policy for Daily Dish

Effective Date: 30th December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Daily Dish uses Matomo Analytics to collect the following types of information about visitors to our website:

  • Device and Browser Information: Includes device type (e.g., desktop, mobile), browser type and version, operating system, screen resolution, and platform details.
  • Usage Data: Includes the time and date of visits, pages viewed, and actions taken (e.g., clicks and scrolls).
  • Performance Metrics: Information about loading times and server performance (e.g., network performance and server response times).
  • Geolocation Data: General location data derived from your IP address (e.g., country or region).
  • Cookies: Cookies may be used to track your preferences and improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used to:

  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
  • Monitor and improve website performance.
  • Enhance the user experience by optimizing content and features.
  • Ensure the security and functionality of our website.

3. Data Storage and Retention

All data collected through Matomo Analytics is securely stored and managed in accordance with GDPR-compliant practices. Data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.

4. Your Data Rights

You have the following rights concerning your data:

  • Access: You can request details of the data we collect about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of tracking by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser or by contacting us directly.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies help us understand your preferences and improve our content. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

Daily Dish does not share your data with third parties. All analytics are processed through Matomo, which is hosted in a secure environment and does not transmit data to external services.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
